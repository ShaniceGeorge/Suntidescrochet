Order Cancellation Policy

Can I Cancel My Order?

We understand that sometimes plans change. If you need to cancel an order, please review our cancellation criteria and follow the steps below.

Criteria for Canceling an Order

  • The order has not yet been shipped.
  • The cancellation request is made within 24 hours of placing the order.
  • Customized or personalized items cannot be canceled once production has started.
  • If the order has already been processed for shipping, you may need to request a return instead.

How to Cancel an Order

  1. Email us at support@suntidescrochet.com with your order number and reason for cancellation.
  2. Alternatively, you can call our support team at +1 (555) 123-4567.
  3. If your order is eligible for cancellation, we will process your request within 24 hours and issue a refund if applicable.
  4. If the order has already shipped, we will provide instructions for returning the package (return shipping fees may apply).

Refund Policy for Canceled Orders

If your order is successfully canceled before shipment, a full refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Orders canceled after shipment may be eligible for a partial refund depending on the return policy.
