Shipping Overview

At The SunTides Crochet, we offer fast and reliable shipping to ensure your crochet items arrive on time. Below are the details about our shipping methods, pricing, and delivery times.

Available Shipping Options

Standard Shipping

Price: $5.00

Delivery Time: 5-7 business days

This option provides reliable delivery at an affordable price. Suitable for most orders.

Expedited Shipping

Price: $15.00

Delivery Time: 2-3 business days

If you need your items quickly, our expedited shipping will get your order to you faster.

Overnight Shipping

Price: $30.00

Delivery Time: 1 business day

For urgent orders, we offer overnight shipping. Get your items the next day, guaranteed!

Shipping Policies

Order Processing: Orders are processed within 24 hours of payment confirmation. Orders placed after 3 PM EST will be processed the next business day.

International Shipping: Currently, we offer shipping only within the United States. We are working on expanding our services to include international delivery in the near future.

Tracking Your Order: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor the status of your shipment.

Free Shipping: Free standard shipping is available on orders over $50. This offer applies to U.S. orders only.
