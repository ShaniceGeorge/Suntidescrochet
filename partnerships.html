Partnerships & Collaborations

Let's Work Together!

At The SunTides Crochet, we love collaborating with brands, designers, and creatives who share our passion for handcrafted crochet pieces. Whether you're a business looking to feature our products or an independent designer interested in showcasing your work, we would love to hear from you!

Opportunities for Collaboration

Brand Collaborations

We welcome brands that align with our values to collaborate on unique projects, limited-edition collections, and co-branded merchandise.

Retail Partnerships

Interested in selling our products in your store? We offer wholesale partnerships and bulk ordering options.

Social Media Influencers

Love crochet? Join our ambassador program and promote our designs on Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest.

Workshops & Events

Collaborate with us on hosting crochet workshops, pop-up events, or online webinars for crochet enthusiasts.

Independent Designers

We believe in supporting independent designers and giving them a platform to showcase their creativity. If you're a crochet artist or pattern designer, we would love to feature your work!

How We Support Independent Designers:

  • Feature your crochet designs on our website.
  • Promote your work through our social media channels.
  • Offer exclusive collaboration opportunities.
  • Provide a commission-based program for your designs.

If you're interested in collaborating, send us your portfolio and a short introduction to collaborate@suntidescrochet.com.

Get in Touch

If you're interested in a partnership or collaboration, we'd love to hear from you! Fill out our inquiry form or email us at partnerships@suntidescrochet.com.
